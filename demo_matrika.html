About Matrika Medical OCR

  • Mission: Digitize healthcare by training AI agents to extract structured data from medical prescriptions
  • Technology: OpenEnv framework + Tesseract OCR + FastAPI backend
  • Tasks: 3 difficulty levels with 15 real prescription images and ground truth annotations
  • Impact: Helps hospitals and pharmacies worldwide digitize paper prescriptions efficiently
  • Accuracy: Reward system based on text accuracy and field completeness
